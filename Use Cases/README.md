# Use Cases & Example Applications

The Solar Drive platform enables direct powering of practical DC loads using available energy sources.
Below are tested and recommended application examples grouped by common operating ranges.

---

# 📁 5V Systems

5V systems are ideal for compact electronics, USB-powered devices, embedded hardware, and portable applications.

---

## 📱 phone-charger/

### Overview

Use Solar Drive to power USB charging modules for mobile phones and other small rechargeable gadgets that operate on 5V input.

### Applications

* Smartphones
* Power banks
* Bluetooth accessories

### Benefits

* Direct daytime charging
* Portable emergency use
* Renewable low-power charging solution

---

## 💡 usb-led-light/

### Overview

Drive USB LED lamps and low-power lighting systems directly from available source power without needing complex conversion stages.

### Applications

* Study lamps
* Emergency lights
* Camping lights

### Benefits

* Instant lighting during source availability
* Low energy consumption
* Portable lighting option

---

# 📁 12V Systems

12V systems are ideal for medium-power loads, field systems, mobility accessories, and off-grid utilities.


---

## 💡 off-grid-lighting/

### Overview

Use Solar Drive for standalone lighting systems where direct power can be used without dependence on grid electricity.

### Applications

* Cabin lighting
* Outdoor pathway lights
* Shed lighting

### Benefits

* Useful in remote areas
* Reduces grid dependency
* Simple low-maintenance lighting

---

## 🌀 solar-fan/

### Overview

Directly operate 12V DC fans for airflow, cooling, and ventilation during active energy availability.

### Applications

* Ventilation fan
* Greenhouse fan
* Room circulation fan

### Benefits

* Improves comfort naturally
* Supports agricultural airflow
* Efficient daytime cooling use

---

## 💧 dc-water-pump/

### Overview

Power compact DC pumps for water movement, irrigation, and circulation systems when sufficient source power is available.

### Applications

* Garden watering
* Small fountain
* Hydroponic circulation

### Benefits

* Useful for irrigation systems
* Supports water recirculation
* Practical renewable utility load
